CA Genie Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2025

At CA Genie, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share the data you provide through our app, as well as the measures we take to protect it. By using CA Genie, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information from students to help connect you with career opportunities, provide mentorship and lecture services, and improve our app. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when creating an account or completing your profile.
  • Educational and Career Data: Details such as your academic qualifications, certifications, skills, resume, work experience, and career preferences.
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you use CA Genie, including login times, features accessed (e.g., mentorship sessions or lectures), and interactions within the app.
  • Device Information: Technical details like your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Matching You with Employers: To share your profile with companies looking to hire candidates whose skills and qualifications align with their job requirements.
  • Providing Mentorship and Lectures: To offer educational content, personalized guidance, and career development support through mentorship sessions and lectures.
  • Enhancing Our Services: To analyze your data and provide additional features or services within CA Genie, such as career tips, job alerts, or personalized recommendations.
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, or support messages related to your account, opportunities, or scheduled mentorship/lecture sessions.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your data in these specific ways:

  • With Companies Hiring: Your personal and professional information may be shared with employers or recruiters to help you find job opportunities that match your profile.
  • For Our Internal Use: We may use your data to develop and provide other services within CA Genie, including mentorship and lecture offerings, to support your career and educational goals.
  • Job Vacancy Aggregation: We post job vacancies sourced from other platforms on CA Genie to provide a single app for all opportunities. These postings may include email IDs or LinkedIn profiles of hiring contacts. If you are a recruiter or company and wish us to remove your post from our app, please contact us at cagenieofficial@gmail.com, and we will ensure its removal within a maximum of 48 hours.

We do not sell your data to third parties or make it publicly available. Your information is shared only with companies for recruitment purposes or used by us to improve your experience with CA Genie. We may also disclose your data if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Your Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Edit Your Profile: Update or modify your information through the app at any time.
  • Opt-Out of Sharing: You can choose to stop sharing your data with companies by adjusting your account settings or contacting us at cagenieofficial@gmail.com.
  • Delete Your Account: Request the removal of your account and data by reaching out to us directly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services, including mentorship and lectures. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information, except where retention is required for legal or operational reasons.

7. Third-Party Services

CA Genie may link to external websites or services (e.g., employer portals or platforms hosting job vacancies). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, so we recommend reviewing their policies.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy as needed. If significant changes occur, we'll notify you via the app or email. Your continued use of CA Genie after updates indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

If you have questions or requests about your data or this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us:

Email: cagenieofficial@gmail.com
